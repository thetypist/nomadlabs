Stories

Coliving

Let’s face it, most of our lives are complete chaos! Trying to juggle work, health, fitness, travel, friends, family, money…Even writing it is exhausting!

We help take the edge off a lot of that by not only giving you an instant base camp to live like a local but to also help develop a great routine, network and life balance.

Coworking

Coworking is everywhere, but we go a step further (or at least we think we do). We don’t let you just show up and procrastinate, oh no! When you stay with us we keep you productive and happy by breaking the day up into work / live / play / learn / rest segments which encourage creative development and help you get loads done without feeling exhausted..tricky right ;).

Cocreating

We know that truly innovative ideas are not born from what we already know but from prototyping, testing, and iterating.

This is why each of our labs provide access to 3D printers, VR equipment and other useful and fun stuff to help fast-track your ideas (or at the very least, teach you something new).

We also host regular enrichment workshops about future tech, lifestyle design, and the more popular craft beer tastings.

Community

When you stay with NomadLabs you don’t just get a dope place to live and work, you also join our diverse community of international innovators and creators. Whether it’s in a lab or at one of our exclusive networking events, you’re going to meet and get to know some amazing people!

Some of you will become great friends, and others might never speak to again. But one things for sure, you’re going to learn a lot from eachother, have some awesome conversations and expand your network in ways which might just change your life.
