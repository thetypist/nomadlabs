Frequently Asked Questions

Why don’t I just AirBnB? I mean, that’s how I always do it...
You could! It will just be a different experience. Each NomadLab property is designed to balance privacy, community and productivity. So it’s easy to meet great people, and still have quiet productive time. Life is more fun when sharing is involved. You can also expect certain things reliably, since we manage each location. From kitchen utensils, to battle-tested internet, to amazing community managers hosting events. Of course, there will also be many spontaneous moments unique to your stay Furthermore, we haven’t heard of any Airbnbs which have in-house prototyping fabrication labs and weekly tech workshops, that alone is a pretty good reason to stay with us ;).
What do you mean by coliving?
It’s a balance of your private refuge plus design-inspired social areas. Imagine if you had a boutique apartment in awesome location and renovated it to have fully furnished private rooms and bathrooms, a chef style kitchen, co-working spaces, and some other awesome areas to relax and hangout. Well that’s exactly what we’ve done. The engaging and social atmosphere is what makes coliving unique. There’s a communal dinner once a week (at least!) and other random events as they come up (always plenty). But our programs and community is all choice based, so there’s community when you want it, and privacy as you need it.
Can I print stuff there?
Yes, but we trust you not to abuse this system otherwise we will have to start referring guests to printing shops.
I need really fast Internet…I work online!
Cool, that’s exactly what we are designed for…so we gotcha covered! Rest assured that our Wi-Fi will be the fastest available.
How many people will be staying in the lab?
You’ll find up to 6 people staying at our Flagship Brisbane location and around 10 people working there on Weekdays.
